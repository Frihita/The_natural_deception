THE NATURAL DECEPTION

Lemon or capsicum — which contains more Vitamin C?

Capsicum actually has nearly double the Vitamin C compared to lemon per 100 grams.

Don't assume sour means more Vitamin C.

Honey or jaggery — which is the healthier alternative to sugar?

Neither is a 'health food.' Both are sugar sources. Jaggery has trace minerals, but the calorie and sugar content are almost identical.

Natural sugar is still sugar.
